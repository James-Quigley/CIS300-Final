Meeting Info

The Computer Science Club meets several times a week to allow members with even the most rigorous schedulues to make meetings, and to provide a variety of different types of interactions with coding.

Meeting Name Day Time Description
General Meeting Wednesday 1:30pm Watching educational videos and going over club plans/policy
Interview Practice: Code Challenge Wednesday 6:00pm Prep for even the scariest of technical interview with code challenge practice
Project Time Friday 3pm Work on one of the club's current projects, homework, or just learn something new
