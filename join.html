Join Us!

We're always looking for new members, so join us! Here are some of the many ways you can get involved:

  1. Slack is an IRC like chat platform built for the modern age. It is heavily used in the Software industry, so get accustomed now! Hang out with the other members and talk about classes, homework, club or personal projects and more.

  2. OrgSync is where you can discover all of the clubs that ASU has to offer. You can find a calendar of events, subscribe to our email newsletter, and find other clubs.

  3. Our GitHub organization is where we keep all of our code and projects. Gain experience working on open source projects by forking, cloning, and contributing to one of our many projects.
