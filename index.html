Computer Science Club

The Computer Science Club at ASU is based out of the Polytechnic campus, and focuses on project based learning in order to prepare members for their careers. Contributions to large open source projects, interview practice, code challenges, participating in hackathons, and the occasional pick-up soccer match are all frequent activities here.
